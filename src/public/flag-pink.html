Semaphore

They say that there are ocean people, and mountain people. Extroverts love the open air, and introverts love protection of the earth.

I disagree. Nobody can startle you on the water. You can be in company, and yet, entirely alone, signaling to others from miles away. The ocean is an indifferent sanctuary and a lonely kind of freedom.

In this station, you will use Flag Semaphore to communicate with the other team, which is located down the street from you.

Flag semaphore uses flags and body positions to communicate letters (and numbers). Flags and a physical copy of the Semaphore Alphabet are provided.

There are three parts to this, from easiest to hardest. Proceed to the next one when you are ready.
